My Story and goals with Web Design...

My name is Jonathan Silva and I am a college graduate working as a city mailroom worker looking to expand my skillset through learning the fundamentals behind web design. During my last years in college I was apprehensive towards the long process required for learning the subject, but the pandemic allow me the time to jump in to the subject. Although being a mailroom worker for the city has the benefit of having job security, I became bored with my normal routine and wanted to shake things up. When I learned of MEDA's Mission Techies program I jumped at the opportunity of undertaking the structured curriculum I always desired. My goals for the program lies within improving my employment prospects and learning valuable skills that will make me an asset for potential employers. A personal goal of mine lies within helping local buisnesses and tradeworkers gain a better foothold to establish their buisnesses by helping them develop websites and social media pages to better advertise to a larger network.
